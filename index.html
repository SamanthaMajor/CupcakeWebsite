About Nicole Major

What started out as baking cupcakes for class events in Elementarty school quickly turned into one of my favorite hobbies. Baking has always put my mind at ease after a stressful day. Friends and Family quickly caught on to this and started ordering cupcakes and cakes when they themselves couldn't find time to bake. It didn't take long for my creativity to kick in. I found that cupcakes didn't have to be just for Kids Parties. I stared developing my receipies for Boozy Cakes to market towards adult parties.
